Records

This section highlights some of the most impressive records and achievements of Lionel Messi throughout his illustrious career.

Goal-Scoring Records

Messi holds numerous goal-scoring records, including being the all-time top scorer for FC Barcelona, with over 600 goals in official competitions. He also holds the record for the most goals scored in a single season for Barcelona, with 73 goals in the 2011-2012 season. Additionally, Messi is the all-time top scorer in La Liga, with over 450 goals.

Ballon d'Or Awards

Messi has won the prestigious Ballon d'Or award multiple times, recognizing him as the best football player in the world. He has won the award a record-breaking 7 times, solidifying his status as one of the greatest footballers of all time.

International Records

Messi is also the all-time top scorer for the Argentina national team, with over 80 goals. He has represented Argentina in multiple FIFA World Cup tournaments and Copa America competitions, contributing significantly to the team's success.
