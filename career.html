Career

This section provides an overview of Lionel Messi's career, including his early beginnings, rise to stardom, and achievements across different clubs.


Early Career

Messi began his football journey at a young age, joining the youth academy of Newell's Old Boys in Argentina. His exceptional talent was evident from the start, soon big clubs started showing interest in him,because of his incredible skills and potential. Even though at that time the best club in the world Real Madrid wanted Lionel Messi to embrace their club with talent and exceptional skills.


Barcelona

After rejecting Real Madrid in 2004, Messi joined FC Barcelona's senior team, where he would spend the majority of his career. Where his incredible dribbling skills, vision, and goal-scoring ability quickly did not make him a standout player in the club's history only , but it made people started wondering if he's the greatest of all time.


Paris Saint-Germain

In 2021 after decesive betrayel from Barcelona's president Laporta , Messi made a high-profile move to Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) after spending over two decades at Barcelona. He continued to showcase his talent and contribute to the team's success, but it wouldn't take a long time for Messi to leave Paris-Saint-Germain.


Inter Miami

In 2023, Messi made a surprising move to Inter Miami in Major League Soccer (MLS) due to lack of happiness and support from Paris-Saint-Germain ,because neither Messi or nor PSG's Players and staff had emotional support from each other, so Messi decided to move to a club that would provide him with the support and happiness he needed. This marked a new chapter in his career, as he brought his world-class skills to the United States and continued to make an impact on the footballing world.

International Career

Messi has also had a remarkable international career with the Argentina national team. He has represented his country in multiple Copa America and FIFA World Cup tournaments, earning numerous accolades along the way.
